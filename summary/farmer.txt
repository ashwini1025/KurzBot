The entire life of the farmers is spent in cultivating their fields. We all know that India was earlier known as the golden bird, there was no dearth of food and money in the country, and the farmers were overall happy. The horrible fact which came to light was that along with the farmers of Vidarbha, the farmers from Maharashtra and Andhra Pradesh, including other states in the country were found doing suicides. According to the statistics, in the year 2009, total 17,368 farmers committed suicide and on an average the annual rate of farmers committing suicide is being recorded as 10,000 per annum currently.
